Donate to Conservancy!

As a not-for-profit charity, Conservancy relies on support from the public to continue its work. Please give generously to support Conservancy's work.

Become a Supporter Now!

The best way to donate to Conservancy is via our official Supporter program, which earns a t-shirt and other surprise Supporter-only perks throughout the year.

But, there are many other great ways to donate to Conservancy as well, listed below:

Donate by paper check

Send paper check donations, drawn in USD, to:

Brooklyn, NY 11201-3548
USA


Donate monthly via PayPal (including Visa, Mastercard, AMEX or ACH)

Donate via Flattr

Flattr is a social micro-payment system that allows users to make donations via the web. Flattr does not charge any processing fees on donations to Conservancy. If you are a Flattr user and you would like to "flattr" Conservancy with a donation, click on the button below:

Donate a Portion of your eBay Sales

Through the eBay/PayPal Giving Fund program, you can donate a portion of your eBay sale proceeds to Software Freedom Conservancy! You can also donate directly through the PayPal Giving fund.


Donate a Portion of your Amazon Purchases

Through Amazon's Smile program, Amazon will donate to Conservancy for your purchases there. Just sign up through the following this link.

Donate by wire transfer

Donations are accepted by wire transfer. Please contact <accounting@sfconservancy.org> for wire transfer instructions, and include in your email the following information:

  • The country of origin of your wire transfer.
  • The native currency of your donation.

Note that Conservancy can accept donations by wire in CAD, EUR, GBP, and USD without currency conversion fees!


Donate stock

Conservancy can accept donation of stocks! Please contact <accounting@sfconservancy.org> for details.

Donate to Our Member Projects

Finally, Conservancy also maintains directed donation programs for its member projects. Donation links for these directed donation programs are typically found on the individual websites of our members.

Donations Are Usually Tax-Deductible!

Software Freedom Conservancy, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) organization incorporated in New York, and donations made to it are fully tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
