Supporter Night 2015

The easiest way to attend Conservancy's Annual Supporter Night — hosted this year on the evening of 30 January 2015 in Brussels, Belgium — is to become an annual supporter now, and chose “Yes” to the related question during signup.

Annual supporters are all invited!

Conservancy Supporter Night 2015

Who
Everyone: just sign up & pay below! — but Conservancy Annual Supporters attend gratis.
When
Friday 30 January 2015 at 18:30 (6:30PM)
Where
Drug Opera Restaurant at Rue Grétry 51, 1000 Brussels, Belgium (map)
What
Full prix fixe dinner (one drink included)
Why
Enjoy a meal while meeting and discussing Free Software with fellow Conservancy enthusiasts and Bradley and Karen from Conservancy's executive team (and the Free as in Freedom audcast).

Conservancy's Annual Supporter Night will be held this year on the evening of Friday 30 January 2015 in Brussels, Belgium (which is the night before FOSDEM 2015 begins).

Space for this exclusive dinner is limited so you must reserve your slot via one of the following means:

  • Become an annual Supporter of Conservancy now, and choose “Yes” to the appropriate question.
  • If you are already an annual Supporter, email <dinner@sfconservancy.org> to reserve your ticket.
  • Pay US$40 (or more, if you want to support Conservancy further) below.
  • Pay €50 (or sign up as an annual supporter for €120) at the door on 30 January 2015. (However, space is limited; the only way to ensure your slot is to sign up in advance as an annual Supporter or below.)

The Supporter Night dinner includes a three-course meal and one drink. Attendees will be able to choose from beef or vegetarian entrees. We have discussed other dietary needs with the venue, and they have committed to do their best to accommodate other dietary needs. If you'd like, please contact us at <dinner@sfconservancy.org> to discuss your dietary needs.
