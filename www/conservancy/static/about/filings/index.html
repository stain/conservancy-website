Public Filings

Like all USA 501(c)(3) non-profits, the Conservancy files an annual Form 990 and, as a non-profit in the State of New York, files an annual CHAR-500 with New York State. Below, Conservancy makes available these filings for public inspection, in reverse chronological order:

  • Fiscal Year 2012 (from 2012-03-01 through 2013-02-28)

    For FY 2012, Conservancy's due date for filing its Form 990, CHAR 500, and related paperwork is currently 2013-07-15.

  • Fiscal Year 2011 (from 2011-03-01 through 2012-02-29)
    • FY 2011 Annual Report (PDF) (source in ODP format is also available).
    • FY 2011 Federal Form 990 & 990-T (PDF)
    • FY 2011 New York State CHAR-500 (PDF)
    • FY 2011 Independent Auditor's Report (PDF)
  • Fiscal Year 2010 (from 2010-03-01 through 2011-02-28)
