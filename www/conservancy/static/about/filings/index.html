Public Filings

Like all USA 501(c)(3) non-profits, the Conservancy files an annual Form 990 and, as a non-profit in the State of New York, files an annual CHAR-500 with New York State. Below, Conservancy makes available these filings for public inspection, in reverse chronological order:

  • Fiscal Year 2011

    Conservancy's FY 2011 ended on 2012-02-29. Conservancy's current filing deadline for FY 2011 Form 990 and CHAR-500 is 2013-02-01.

    Specifically, Conservancy's initial deadline for the was 2012-07-16. The IRS and NYS granted Conservancy's application for a three-month filing extension until 2012-10-15. On 2012-09-25, the IRS additionally granted a second three-month filing extension until 2013-01-15. Finally, the IRS unilaterally granted deadline extensions for all counties impacted by Hurricane Sandy until 2013-02-01.
